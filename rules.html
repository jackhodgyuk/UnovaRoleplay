Unova Roleplay Official BIBLE of Rules

Welcome to Unova Roleplay, a serious FiveM roleplay server focused on immersive, realistic and story-driven roleplay.

1. Community Standards

  • Strictly 18+
  • Functional microphone required
  • Clipping software required
  • No harassment, racism, sexism or discrimination
  • No advertising other communities
  • No cheating or exploiting

2. Character & Roleplay Rules

  • Remain in character at all times
  • No Fail RP
  • No Metagaming
  • No Powergaming
  • No ERP or terrorism RP
  • Value your life realistically

3. Combat Rules

  • No RDM or VDM
  • Hostile RP requires initiation
  • Guns are a last resort
  • No unrealistic vehicle usage
  • Green Zones must be respected

4. New Life Rule

If your character dies or enters a coma, you forget the last 15 minutes leading up to the event.

5. Whitelisted Jobs

  • No impersonating Police, UHS, Fire or Government
  • Corruption requires staff approval
  • Police equipment cannot be stolen

6. Final Notes

Staff reserve the right to interpret rules based on context. Rules may change at any time.
